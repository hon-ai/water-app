What you're testing

  • The editor itself — prose-first, with subtle live underlines for passive voice, repetition, weak verbs, sentence-length variance.
  • The ambient pill engine — a council of five personas (Echo, Architect, Editor, Cartographer, Chorus) that surface small noticings as you pause.
  • The rabbit-hole expansion — click any pill to fan out four counter-angles (closer / wider / opposite / deeper).
  • Per-scene metadata — character + location linking that sharpens the council's voice.

What we want to hear

  • What pills felt useful. What pills felt like noise.
  • Friction in setup, in switching providers, in the first hour.
  • Anything that looked broken, anything that surprised you good, anything you wished worked differently.
